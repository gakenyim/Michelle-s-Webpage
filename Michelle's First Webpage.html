Pilau.

Ingredients.

  • 1 kg of meat.
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 medium onions, chopped and cubed.
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic and ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste.
  • 3 tablespoons of pilau masala.
  • 1tablespoon of salt.
  • Cooking oil

Preparation.

  1. Step 1:Begin by boiling your meat till tender.
  2. Step 2:Soak your rice in warm water for 10 minutes.
  3. Step 3:Heat cooking oil in a sufuria and add in your onions. Saute them till golden brown.
  4. Step 4:Add in the garlic and ginger paste.
  5. Step 5:Cook for 2 minutes and add in your meat.
  6. Step 6:After 3 minutes,add in your tomato paste and pilau masala and stir.
  7. Step 7:Add 4 cups of water and allow to simmer then add in your salt.
  8. Step 8:Add in your rice,lower your heat and cover with aluminium foil then bring the mixture to boil.

Cook for 20 minutes.Serve and enjoy with your choice of beverage.
