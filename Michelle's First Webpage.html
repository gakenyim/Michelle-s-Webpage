PILAU.

Ingredients.

1 kg of meat.
2 cups of rice.
2 medium onions, chopped and cubed.
1 tablespoon of garlic and ginger paste.
1 tablespoon of tomato paste.
3 tablespoons of pilau masala.
Cookingoil.
1tablespoon of salt.

Preparation.

Step 1:Begin by boiling your meat till tender.
Step 2:Soak your rice in warm water for 10 minutes.
Step 3:Heat cooking oil in a sufuria and add in your onions. Saute them till golden brown.
Step 4: Add in the garlic and ginger paste.
Step 5:Cook for 2 minutes and add in your meat.
Step 6:After 3 minutes,add in your tomato paste and pilau masala and stir.
Step 7:Add 4 cups of water and allow to simmer then add in your salt.
Step 8:Add in your rice,lower your heat and cover with aluminium foil then bring the mixture to boil.

Cook for 20 minutes.Serve and enjoy with your choice of beverage.
